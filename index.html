✦ FAQ

Got questions?

Is Cyanix AI free?
Yes — completely free. All four models, all eight tools, web search, image generation, and full chat history at no cost. Complex Thinking is capped at 50 messages/day to keep it sustainable.
What AI models power Cyanix?
Cyanix runs on Meta's Llama 4 family: Maverick (128K, most capable), Scout (128K, balanced), Flash (64K, fastest), and Pro (128K, highest output). All served via Groq for ultra-fast inference.
How is Cyanix different from ChatGPT?
Cyanix is built for everyday adults — not just developers. It has built-in tools for presentations, resumes, invoices, budgets, meal plans and more. It shows its full reasoning (Complex Thinking), has real-time web search, and is completely free with no credit system or usage caps on core features.
Is my data private?
Your conversations are saved locally in your browser first (localStorage) then synced securely to your account. Cyanix does not sell or share your data. You can delete any chat at any time from inside the app.
Can Cyanix make real slideshows I can present?
Yes. Cyanix generates interactive slideshows directly inside the chat — navigate slides, go fullscreen and present with arrow keys, and export the content as a text outline. Just click "Presentation" in the sidebar, describe your topic, and it builds it for you.
Do I need an account?
An account is needed to save your chat history and access all features. Sign up takes 10 seconds with email, Google, GitHub, Discord, or Microsoft.
